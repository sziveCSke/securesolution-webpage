Secure Solution Europe is an agile multi-trade company specialising in security systems (access control, door entry, intercom, CCTV, automated gates and shutters) offering a wide range of services including the installation, commission, maintenance, repair and emergency callouts with several years’ experience.

We pride ourselves on our outstanding professional service provided by our knowledgable engineers and our attention to detail which takes our service delivery beyond the expected. We are dedicated to provide high quality services to our customers who can rely on us.

We strive to implement a long term relationship with our clients, based on high quality, high safety, timely service and an anticipation of their needs.

We offer fast, efficient and reliable services, covering Greater London and the Home Counties.

We aim to provide our customers with a “First Visit Fix” - meaning our engineers attend the property, identify, diagnose and remediate the fault. In some cases we need to re-attend when a specialist part is required or the work takes much longer than it was originally anticipated, but the new appointment is arranged for the client’s convenience.

Our highly skilled, committed workforce helps to tailor our services to meet our clients’ individual needs. We always try to find ways to bring the most advanced and beneficial solutions according to their requirements.

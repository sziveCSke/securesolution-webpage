Automated Gates and Shutters

Assessment

We take both your security and safety seriously therefore we offer a service to assess your automated gate and advise if there is any upgrade needed in order to keep your gates in good operative order and to comply with health and safety requirements.

Based on the assessment we can provide you with a quotation for the necessary improvement works.

For further information please contact us.

Force Testing

With New Health and Safety Directive in force, all automated gates and shutters must be “FORCE TESTED” to comply with the current legislation. Also we can offer a service to force test your automated gates ensuring that the closing and opening forces are correct according to the British/European Standards and HSE guidance. For further information please contact us.

Preventative Maintenance

Prevention is better than cure, regular maintenance is always cheaper than repair work. All systems require maintenance and it is recommended to be carried out at regular intervals depending on the conditions and the volume of usage. Scheduled checking of automated gates and shutters ensures safe and smooth running of the system and prolongs the operational life of them. Also it helps to spot and mitigate any potential problems before they become a major one.

Reasonable gate maintenance agreements are available for all types of automated gates and shutters. For further information please contact us.

Repairs

We repair any type of electric gate and shutter parts to be as near to their original condition as possible in order to keep your gates and shutters safe and in good operative order.

On most occasions the repair is carried out on our first visit. However, if the repair is more involved and needs replacement parts, we will complete the survey and the visit will be followed by either a quote or a comprehensive report for repairing the issue.

For further information please do not hesitate to contact us.
